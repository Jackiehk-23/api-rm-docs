## Testing Wallets

:::note
- Only limited wallets provide testing accounts — most are in beta (unstable).
- WeChat Pay is the only wallet usable with a real production wallet in **both** production and sandbox environments.
- Recommend using WeChat Pay in sandbox for one round of testing, then move to production for other wallets.
:::
